# Character Animations

## Idle
idle breathing, slight body movement

## Walk
walking forward, left foot then right foot

## Run
running fast, arms pumping

## Jump
jumping up high, arms raised

## Attack
sword slash attack, quick swing

## Death
falling down defeated, collapsing

## Hit
taking damage, flinching backwards

## Cast Spell
casting magic spell, hands glowing with energy

## Crouch
crouching down into defensive position

## Climb
climbing upward, reaching and pulling
